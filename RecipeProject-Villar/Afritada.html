Afritada


Description:

Afritada is a popular Filipino dish known for its hearty and comforting flavors. This tomato-based stew typically features tender pieces of meat (commonly chicken, pork, or beef) simmered with potatoes, carrots, bell peppers, and other vegetables in a flavorful sauce. Afritada is beloved for its rich taste and vibrant colors, making it a favorite dish for gatherings and family meals alike.

Difficulty:

Moderate

Time to Prepare:

Preparation: 15 minutes
Estimated Cooking Time: 45 minutes
Total: 1 hour

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg chicken, beef, or pork, cut into serving pieces
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 2 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup chicken or beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce (patis)
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Green peas (optional, for garnish)

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Saut√© garlic and onions until fragrant.
  2. Add the meat pieces and cook until lightly browned on all sides.
  3. Pour in the tomato sauce, chicken or beef broth, soy sauce, fish sauce, sugar, and bay leaf. Stir to combine.
  4. Cover and simmer for about 30 minutes or until the meat is tender.
  5. Add the potatoes, carrots, and bell peppers. Continue to simmer for another 10-15 minutes or until the vegetables are cooked through.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. If desired, add green peas for extra color and flavor.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice and enjoy your delicious Afritada!
